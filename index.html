A journey through the ancient practices of healing, beauty, and spiritual maintenance passed down through generations of Black and Brown women.

Before the apothecary, before the dispensary, there was the kitchen. The windowsill crowded with small jars. The grandmother who knew what the body needed before the body knew itself. This is where medicine lived — unwritten, embodied, passed hand to hand.
